About Buhay Roofing

Your Trusted Roofing Partner Since 2009

Founded in Manila, Buhay Roofing Solutions has grown from a small family enterprise into one of Metro Manila's most dependable roofing contractors. We understand the demands of the Philippine climate — from intense heat to powerful typhoons — and we build roofs that last.

Licensed & Insured — PCAB-accredited contractors with full liability coverage for every project.

Typhoon-Proof Standards — All installations follow PAG-ASA wind-load guidelines for maximum durability.

2-Year Workmanship Warranty — Every completed project is backed by our quality guarantee.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Roofing Services

From new builds to emergency repairs, our team of skilled roofers is equipped to handle any challenge — big or small.

Roof Installation

Complete new roof installations using premium materials — Corrugated G.I. sheets, long-span roofing, and clay tiles — built to withstand Philippine weather conditions.

Roof Repair

Fast and reliable repairs for leaks, damaged panels, loose flashings, and storm damage. We respond within 24–48 hours for urgent situations.

Roof Inspection

Thorough roof health assessments to identify weak points, potential leaks, and structural issues before they escalate — ideal before typhoon season.

Waterproofing

Professional waterproofing solutions for flat roofs, concrete decks, and terraces using high-grade elastomeric coatings and membrane systems.

Roof Insulation

Reduce indoor heat and energy costs with our roof insulation services — keeping your home cooler during summer and protected during the rainy season.

Commercial Roofing

Specialized roofing solutions for warehouses, commercial buildings, and industrial facilities. We handle large-scale projects with precision and efficiency.
